About Us

Hello! Our names are Isabel and Kallie, and we are the founders of this nonprofit organization. We both took interest in special needs classrooms in our area when we noticed that they are often not funded adequately to promote a stimulating learning environment.


We talked to special needs teachers in our area and found that many schools around the US do not have funds to provide supplies such as stress toys, games, and other learning materials. That's why we created the RAISE project, which aims to raise money for special needs programs.


Join our email list today (located under the Join tab) to help us with our mission, and get notified when we have an ongoing project! Those who participate will also have the opportunity to earn service hours!
